Welcome to Little Racer

The object is to get your race car to the finish line first. Player 1 uses the right arrow to move their race car. Player 2 uses the 'd' key to move their race car. Press the "Start Race" button when ready.

Player 1 Score = 0

Player 2 Score = 0

Race Starts in 5
